An undertaking by Sulaiman Khan Ghori to advance space flight technologies by reducing barriers in fundamental technology. We are developing an open source rocket engine. Our end goal is to seed life in the universe, likely via von Neumann probes. There is much work to be done.

Updates:
  • September 2024: Currently building test stand and first prototype engine.
  • August 2024: Read Rocket Propulsion Elements, Ignition!, Design of Liquid-Propellant Rocket Engines.
  • July 2024: Went to the Alps and decided rockets > saas.
